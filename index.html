What makes Groove different

Built around listening, not listings.

🎵
Listening Rooms
Virtual rooms where collectors spin records together. Drop into a Jazz at Midnight session, discover a new album, and follow the host's shelf — then buy what caught your ear.
Discovery
🤝
Taste Matching
Our algorithm builds a taste profile from your shelf tags and listening history. When you list a record, it surfaces buyers who actually want it — not just the highest bidder.
Commerce
📚
Curated Shelves
Your collection is a statement. Organize by mood, era, genre, or vibe. Share shelves publicly. Follow collectors whose taste you trust. Browse shelves like you'd browse a record store.
Collection
🔄
Trade Circles
List records you want to swap, not just sell. Groove finds multi-party trade loops — you give A what B has, B gives C what they need, C closes the circle. No cash needed.
Trading
🏷️
Contextual Pricing
Prices aren't just condition grades. Groove factors in pressing rarity, regional demand, and recent sale velocity to show you what a record is actually worth right now.
Pricing
🌍
Local First
Before shipping, Groove finds collectors near you who have what you want. Save on shipping, avoid damage risk, and meet people in your local scene. Pickup encouraged.

Product Roadmap

How we get there.

1
MVP — Month 1–3
Foundation
  • User profiles + shelf building
  • Manual record tagging (genre, mood, era)
  • Basic buy/sell listings
  • Discogs API import for catalog data
  • Trust & rating system
2
Beta — Month 4–6
Discovery
  • Taste matching algorithm v1
  • Listening Rooms (live audio via WebRTC)
  • Trade Circle matchmaking
  • Shelf embed widget
  • Mobile app (React Native)
3
Launch — Month 7–9
Commerce
  • Stripe payment integration
  • Contextual pricing engine
  • Local pickup coordination
  • Record grading photo AI
  • Seller analytics dashboard
4
Growth — Month 10+
Scale
  • Record store partnerships
  • Artist-direct releases
  • Pressing run crowdfunding
  • B2B API for shops
  • International expansion
